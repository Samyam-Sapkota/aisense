OUR MISSION

Connecting Nature and Technology for Climate-Smart Innovation

We are an R&D-first venture working at the intersection of climate, agriculture, and biodiversity. Our mission is to design, build, and deploy intelligent systems that bridge natural environments with digital tools — supporting more sustainable, data-driven decisions on the ground. We apply edge AI and nature-inspired algorithms to create responsive, real-time sensing networks for the most challenging outdoor conditions.

  • 🔧 Design efficient, low-power sensor systems for field deployment
  • 🛡️ Build resilient sensing platforms for farming and ecosystems
  • ☁️ Deploy scalable edge-to-cloud systems for adaptive field intelligence

"Our vision is to connect nature with digital insight — advancing ecological understanding and climate resilience, wherever it's needed most."

WHAT WE'RE BUILDING

Current Projects

AI CoExistIntelligent Wildlife Detection & Deterrence

NSF SBIR Phase I–funded technology that integrates AI-enabled cameras to detect, classify, and track wildlife species in real time — enabling humane, adaptive coexistence.

AI-enabled Vision Systems Integrates AI cameras to detect, classify, and track deer, raccoons, and other wildlife in real time.
Adaptive Deterrence Engine Edge-based learning activates beamformed acoustic deterrents for species-specific targeting.
Predictive Reinforcement Loop Reinforcement learning optimizes deterrence timing, reducing habituation over time.
Detect–Deter–Adapt Framework Anticipates animal re-entry patterns for adaptive, humane response strategies.
Open-Source Coexistence Platform Open ecosystem for data, models, and APIs — empowering researchers, farmers, and communities.

Revolutionizing AgricultureAI-Driven Disaster Response

AI-driven system combining satellite, UAV, and IoT data to detect damage, quantify crop loss, and support recovery planning for agricultural disasters.

Rapid Damage Detection Combines satellite, UAV, and IoT data to identify flood, drought, or storm impacts in near-real-time.
Crop Loss Quantification Vegetation indices and AI yield models estimate biomass reduction and financial loss.
Recovery Planning Ranks priority zones by severity and provides adaptive recommendations for replanting.
Policy & Resilience Support Standardized reports for governments and insurers, plus predictive models from past disasters.

Urban FarmingAI Monitoring System

Multi-scale AI monitoring for urban agriculture — from edge-based real-time analysis to cloud diagnostics and personalized grower support.

Edge-Based Real-Time Monitoring Lightweight CNNs on cameras and sensors for continuous crop and environment analysis.
Cloud-Enabled Diagnostics Multi-spectral analysis, anomaly detection, and forecasting across diverse urban farms.

From the Blog

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions on our technology, approach, and how we work with farmers.

How does AI SENSE integrate with existing farming practices?

Our AI solutions complement traditional farming methods, introducing smart automation while keeping farmers at the core of decision-making. We work closely with local agricultural experts to ensure our technology enhances existing practices.

What kind of data do your systems collect?

Our systems collect visual data for weed detection, crop health assessment, and growth monitoring. This includes multispectral imagery and environmental data, all processed through secure AI algorithms to provide actionable insights.

How can farmers access AI SENSE technologies?

We work through partnerships with NARC, local agricultural institutions, and farmer cooperatives. Farmers can access our technologies through these partnerships, receiving comprehensive training and ongoing support.
